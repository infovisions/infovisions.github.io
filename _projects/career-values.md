Funded by [Mozilla Foundation's Responsible Computing Challenge](https://www.mozillafoundation.org/en/responsible-computing-challenge/), this project explores how undergraduate computing students search for careers that align with their personal values and sense of mission — and how they navigate the real tension between economic stability and meaningful work.

We are interviewing two groups:
- Recent graduates working in industry who are reflecting reflecting on how they found (or compromised on) values-aligned work
- Current students actively searching who are capturing how they make decisions today

From this work, we are developing guidance and resources that make visible the often-invisible processes, networks, and opportunities that help students connect their values to their careers
